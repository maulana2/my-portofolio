Akademik Advisor - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Akademik Advisor, an offline application designed to assist users in choosing a college major based on academic performance, collects, uses, and shares your information.

Effective Date: 2023-12-06

Information We Collect

Akademik Advisor does not collect any personal information from users. This includes information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Geographic location
  • IP addresses
  • Device identifiers
  • User behavior data

The application only gathers the academic performance data you manually input, which is solely used for generating the recommended college major.

How We Use the Information We Collect

The academic performance data you enter is processed internally within the application to generate a personalized recommendation for a college major. This data is not stored or transmitted anywhere else.
